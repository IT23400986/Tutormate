FAQs

  • How do I enroll in a class?
    Navigate to the "Classes" section, select your desired class, and click "Enroll."
  • How do I upload payment proof?
    Go to the "Payment" section and upload a clear image or document of the payment receipt.
  • How long does payment verification take?
    Payment verification typically takes up to 24 hours.
